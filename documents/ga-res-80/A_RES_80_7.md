80/7. Report of the International Atomic Energy Agency

The General Assembly,

Having received the report of the International Atomic Energy Agency for 2024,1

Taking note of the statement by the Director General of the Agency, in which he provided additional information on the main developments in the activities of the Agency during 2025,

Recognizing the importance of the work of the Agency,

Recognizing also the cooperation between the United Nations and the Agency and the Agreement governing the relationship between the United Nations and the Agency as approved by the General Conference of the Agency on 23 October 1957 and by the General Assembly in the annex to its resolution 1145 (XII) of 14 November 1957,

1. Takes note with appreciation of the report of the International Atomic Energy Agency;

2. Takes note of resolutions GC(69)/RES/7, entitled “Nuclear and radiation safety”; GC(69)/RES/8, entitled “Nuclear security”; GC(69)/RES/9, entitled “Strengthening the Agency’s activities related to nuclear science, technology and applications”, comprising GC(69)/RES/9 A.2, entitled “Support to the African Union’s Pan African Tsetse and Trypanosomosis Eradication Campaign (AU‑PATTEC)”,
GC(69)/RES/9 A.3, entitled “Zoonotic Disease Integrated Action (ZODIAC) Project”, and GC(69)/RES/9 B, entitled “Nuclear power applications”; GC(69)/RES/10, entitled “Extending the Technical Cooperation Programme for the State of Palestine to the Gaza Strip”; GC(69)/RES/13, entitled “Implementation of the NPT safeguards agreement

between the Agency and the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea”;
GC(69)/RES/14, entitled “Nuclear safety, security and safeguards in Ukraine”;
GC(69)/RES/15, entitled “Application of IAEA safeguards in the Middle East”; and GC(69)/RES/16, entitled “Personnel”, comprising GC(69)/RES/16 A, entitled “Staffing of the Agency’s Secretariat” and GC(69)/RES/16 B, entitled “Women in the Secretariat”; and decisions GC(69)/DEC/10, entitled “Amendment to Article XIV.A of the Statute”; GC(69)/DEC/11, entitled “Promotion of efficiency and effectiveness of the IAEA decision making process”, and GC(69)/DEC/12, entitled “Amendment to Article VI of the Statute”, adopted by the General Conference of the Agency at its sixty-ninth regular session, held from 15 to 19 September 2025;2 and of the statements by the President of the General Conference with regard to the General Conference agenda item 8 entitled “Election of Members to the Board of Governors”; 3 item 15 entitled “Strengthening of the Agency’s technical cooperation activities”; item 16 entitled “Strengthening the Agency’s activities related to nuclear science, technology and applications”; and item 17 entitled “Strengthening the effectiveness and improving the efficiency of Agency safeguards”, all as reflected in the official records of the General Conference;

3. Reaffirms its strong support for the indispensable role of the Agency in encouraging and assisting the development and practical application of atomic energy for peaceful uses, in technology transfer to developing countries and in nuclear safety, verification and security;

4. Appeals to Member States to continue to support the activities of the Agency;

5. Requests the Secretary-General to transmit to the Director General of the Agency the records of the eightieth session of the General Assembly relating to the activities of the Agency.

40th plenary meeting 14 November 2025
